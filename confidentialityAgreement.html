CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

I, the undersigned

  • Shall treat as confidential all information of any nature, pertaining to any patients which during the course of my employment with SYNLAB (hereinafter referred to as “the Company”) may be made available to me and / or which become known to me through any manner whatsoever.
  • Shall not divulge to other third parties any information regarding the Company, its operating methods or its clients

It is specifically recorded that all patient information including test results and account information as confidential. No employee may discuss or divulge the above to any other person, including any other employee/trainee who does not need such information to be made available to them during the normal course of their duties.

A patient is defined as any person who for any reason has a specimen of any kind referred to this or any other SYNLAB Laboratory.
